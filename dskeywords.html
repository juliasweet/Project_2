Disaster Summaries Table Data Fields

Below is a list outining the data fields within the Disaster Summaries table and a desciption and type for each item.


List of Data Fields for the Disaster Summaries Table
Name Title Type Description
disasternumber Disaster Number integer Sequentially assigned number used to designate an event or incident declared as a disaster.
totalnumberiaapproved Total Number IA Approved numeric(25,2) The number of disaster assistance applications that were approved for Individual Assistance (IA)
toalamountihpapproved Total Amount IHP Approved numeric(25,2) The total amount approved for the Individual and Households Program (IHP).
totalamounthaapproved Total Amount HA Approved numeric(25,2) The total amount approved for Housing Assistance (HA) from the Individual and Households Program (IHP).
totalamountonaapproved Total Amount ONA Approved numeric(25,2) The total amount approved for Other Needs Assistance (ONA) from the Individual and Households Program (IHP).
totalobligatedamountpa Total Obligated Amount PA numeric(25,2) The Public Assistance grant funding available to the grantee (State), for sub-grantee approved Project Worksheets
totalobligatedamountcatab Total Obligated Amount Category A and B numeric(25,2) The Public Assistance grant funding available to the grantee (State), for sub-grantee approved Project Worksheets in categories A and B, or Emergency Work. Category A: Debris removal; Category B: Emergency protective measures.
totalobligatedamountcatc2g Total Obligated Amount Catories C to G numeric(25,2) The Public Assistance grant funding available to the grantee (State), for sub-grantee approved Project Worksheets in categories C through G, or Permanent Work. Category C: Roads and bridges; Category D: Water control facilities; Category E: Public buildings and contents; Category F: Public utilities; Category G: Parks, recreational, and other facilities.
paloaddate PA Load Date date The date the PA (Public Assistance) data was updated by NEMIS.
ialoaddate IA Load Date date The date the IA (Individual Assistance) data was updated by NEMIS.
